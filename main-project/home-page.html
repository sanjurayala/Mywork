Welcome to the Department of Computer Science (CS) at Texas Southern University (TSU) and thank you for visiting our website.
On behalf of the students, faculty, and staff, We invite you to explore the many learning and cultural opportunities available at TSU and the CS Department.
In joining our CS department, students experience the TSU commitment to quality education, a special sense of belonging and the chance to develop relationships that will last a lifetime. The CS department offers a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science with a General CS Concentration, a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science with Computer Networks Concentration and a Master of Science degree in Computer Science. Our experienced faculty, small size classes and dynamic CS student organizations allow our students to develop a close and positive working relationship with their instructors and their peers.
Please search our website for the academic program that best meets your needs and contact us if you need more information. We invite you to call the CS department at 713-313-7991 or send an email to Ghemri_lx@tsu.edu with any questions, concerns or comments you may have. We look forward to meeting you at TSU campus at your convenience.
